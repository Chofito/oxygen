[url=https://github.com/Chofito/oxygen] Oxygen [/url] is a pure performance mod. No new items. No gameplay changes. All settings are client-side and independently toggle-able from Mod Config.

Recommended for content-heavy modpacks where boss fights cause frame drops.

Not compatible with Nitrate.

[h1]Features[/h1]
[list]
[*][b]Dust culling[/b]: removes off-screen dust particles and caps total density
[*][b]Dust parallelism[/b]: distributes the dust update loop across multiple CPU cores
[*][b]Projectile parallelism[/b]: runs visual-only projectiles in parallel; damage-dealing and hostile projectiles always run sequentially
[*][b]NPC AI throttling[/b]: reduces update frequency for distant non-boss NPCs; bosses and boss segments are never touched
[*][b]Gore culling[/b]: removes off-screen gore after a configurable timeout
[*][b]Sound throttling[/b]: limits how many times the same sound can play per time window
[*][b]World update throttling[/b]: halves grass and biome spread update rate during boss fights (singleplayer only)
[*][b]Lighting optimization[/b]: skips off-screen light source calls, reducing background thread work
[*][b]Pylon optimization[/b]: faster pylon proximity check
[*][b]Laser ruler rendering[/b]: replaces the vanilla laser ruler renderer with one that uses ~200 draw calls instead of ~14000
[*][b]GC management[/b]: switches to low-latency GC mode during boss fights to reduce stutter (disabled by default)
[*][b]Diagnostics overlay[/b]: per-system frame time breakdown with rolling averages (disabled by default)
[/list]

[h1]Multiplayer[/h1]
Safe for co-op. Visual features run per client independently. NPC throttling is disabled on non-host clients to prevent desync.

[h1]ModCall API[/h1]
Other mods can call Oxygen to exempt specific NPC or projectile types from optimizations:
[c]ModLoader.GetMod("Oxygen").Call("ExemptNPCFromThrottling", npcType)[/c]
[c]ModLoader.GetMod("Oxygen").Call("ExemptProjectileFromHiding", projectileType)[/c]
